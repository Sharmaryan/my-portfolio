Why you should start a business?

september 23, 2021

Startups is the need of India India is a country where unemployment rate is more as compare to other countries. People keeps blaming that this is due to current PM, CM, etc. But this is not the case I think. let's talk about jobs first I think skills and the right mindset matters a lot, there are many peoples, who have wasted so much money on degrees but they have not invested the money into learning the skills to get a job. And they keep on thinking that I have degree but don't have any job offer in my field. They simply don't ask themselves the question ' I deserve this role or not'. Even if they look inside themselves they can clearly see that they don't have any skill needed for that job. What should you do when you are not getting a job, I think you should start investing in yourself by learning the relevant skill for that job or I would say if you are not interested in learing any skill the I think you should start a business, at least give it a try.

You may have seen many peoples around you, who started from small and scaled their small business, even you don't notice it then, but after years you start noticing that they are doing great and also they expanded their business. Incomes tax department found that more than 200 peoples are millionaires in India( how they became millionaire, they started from small stall, or we can say that from small food van). Here point is not that you can be a millionaire or you should also start with stall, even I say this, you will not do, the point is these peoples are not educated and they did very well then think what you can do, being a litterate person.

Chayoos, wow momos, chai sutta bar are the example of some businesses. You may be heard a name or not, but you should know the name of this person and where he is right now, he started a small tea stall and he is popular enterpreneur. He is Prafull Billore, famous with the name of MBA chaiwala. His business turnover is around 3 crore last year, and he expanded to many cities with around more that 10 franchise. He is planning to expand out of India also.

You can start your own business, I know it's not easy to start a business but what else option do you have. You are not getting your dream job, then what will you do. suppose you are interested into sales but you are not getting job, then there are two options, either you go for another job (another field) or start business. I think option two is good. If you start a small business then definetly you can solve your biggest problem of being unemployed, and if it starts making profit then you can't do it alone, may be you have to hire 1, or more people according to the your requirement. So here you solved two problems first you got yourself a job ( you employed yourself) and second you hired someone who was also unemplyed.
