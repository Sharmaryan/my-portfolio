Why you should start a business?

september 23, 2021

Startups is the need of India India is a country where unemployment rate is more as compare to other countries. People keeps blaming that this is due to current PM, CM, etc. But this is not the case I think. I think skills matters, there are many peoples, who have wasted so much money on degrees but they have not invested the money into learning the skills to get a job.

And they keep on thinking that I have degree but don't have any job offer in my field. They simply don't ask themselves the question ' I deserve this role or not'. Even if they look inside themselves they can clearly see that they don't have any skill needed for that job. You can start your own business, I know it's not easy to start a business but what else option do you have. You are not getting your dream job, then what will you do.

suppose you are interested into sales but you are not getting job, then there are two options, either you go for another job (another field) or start business. I think option two is good. If you start a small business then definetly you can solve your biggest problem of being unemployed, and if it starts making profit then you can't do it alone, may be you have to hire 1, or more people according to the your requirement. So here you solved two problems first you got yourself a job ( you employed yourself) and second you hired someone who was also unemplyed.
